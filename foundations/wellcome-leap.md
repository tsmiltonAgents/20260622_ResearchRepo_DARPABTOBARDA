# Wellcome Leap

## Introduction
Wellcome Leap is a US-based 501(c)(3) established in 2020 by the Wellcome Trust to fund DARPA-style "moonshot" health programs (fixed-term, goal-driven, run by program directors who contract a portfolio of "performers"). CEO **Regina Dugan** (former DARPA director). **It is broad** — of ~12–15 programs, only a minority touch infectious disease; most address mental health, addiction, maternal/child & women's health, surgery, aging, computing. Wellcome Leap **does not disclose per-performer amounts** (only program totals).

**Important correction:** the user's "R3 = Reversing Rising Resistance (AMR)" framing is wrong. **R3 = "RNA Readiness + Response"** (pandemic/RNA-manufacturing, co-funded with CEPI). The actual AMR program is **Focused Antibiotics** (2026).

Funding scale: founding endowment reported inconsistently ($250M / $300M); +$335M added Feb 2022 ("over half a billion at work"); per-program budgets typically $40–60M.

## Section 1 — Programmes (infectious-disease relevance flagged)

### R3 — RNA Readiness + Response (and R3 Global) — **pandemic/vaccines (YES)**
$60M program **co-funded with CEPI** (~July 2021), PD Duccio Medini. Goals: exponentially increase biologic products developed/year, and create a self-sustaining globally distributed RNA surge-manufacturing network (target: up to 20B RNA vaccine doses/month during an outbreak). **R3 Global** (~2025) scales the distributed-biofoundry model worldwide.

### Focused Antibiotics — **AMR (YES)**
$50M, 3-yr, launched 2026, PD Jennifer Cohn (ex-GARDP). "Gut-sparing" antibiotic delivery (UTI, LRTI, AOM). Goal: cut antibiotic-driven resistant infections by up to 40%. As of June 2026, soliciting proposals — **no named performers yet**.

### HOPE — Human Organs, Physiology, and Engineering — **immune-adjacent (PARTIAL)**
Wellcome Leap's first program (2020), $50M. Multi-organ platform modeling human immune responses (tumor, autoimmune, infectious disease) to double preclinical predictive value.

### Other programs (NOT infectious-disease)
Delta Tissue, 1kD, MCPsych, In Utero, SAVE, Q4Bio, Dynamic Resilience (with Temasek Trust), Untangling Addiction, CARE, The Missed Vital Sign, VISIBLE, FORM. *(No dedicated rapid-diagnostics/biosurveillance program exists in the portfolio.)*

### Co-funders (more reliably disclosed than performers)

## Key flags
R3 ≠ "Reversing Rising Resistance" (it's RNA Readiness + Response); AMR program is Focused Antibiotics (no performers yet); founding dollar figure and program count conflict across sources; no per-performer amounts disclosed.
