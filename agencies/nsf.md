# NSF — National Science Foundation (Pandemic-Prevention & Infectious-Disease Basic Research)

## Introduction
NSF funds the **fundamental science** of infectious disease and pandemic prevention — complementary to NIH: ecology, evolution, mathematics, computation, engineering, and social dynamics of disease emergence/transmission. Boundary note: **MIDAS** (Models of Infectious Disease Agent Study) is **NIH/NIGMS, not NSF**. Amounts are funds-obligated unless noted; several flagged.

---

## Section 1 — Programmes

### 1.1 Predictive Intelligence for Pandemic Prevention (PIPP)
Launched 2021. **Phase I Development Grants (NSF 21-590):** ~18-month planning grants; outcome **26 projects ≈ $26M** (Sept 2022), ~50 institutions, ~500 researchers (most near $1M). **Phase II Centers (NSF 23-608):** outcome **4 Centers, ~$18M each over 7 yrs** (~$72M; Aug 2024). *(Phase I = 21-590, Phase II = 23-608.)*

### 1.2 Ecology and Evolution of Infectious Diseases (EEID)
Since ~2000 (renamed to add "Evolution" in FY2012, NSF 11-580). Multi-agency: NSF leads; awards may be funded by NSF, NIH (Fogarty/NIGMS/NIAID), USDA/NIFA, UKRI/BBSRC, UK Defra, US-Israel BSF, NSFC China. Requires quantitative/mathematical transmission-dynamics. Recent annual totals ~$27–36.5M, ~8–12 awards; standard awards $1.5M–$3.0M over ≤5 yrs.

### 1.3 RAPID & COVID-19
**RAPID** mechanism (urgent, internal review, historically capped $200K/1 yr). COVID via **DCL NSF 20-052** (Mar 4, 2020). "More than 400" RAPID COVID awards by May 2020 (cleanest figure); a **$75M CARES Act** supplement; "1,200 awards / $200M+" (Dec 2020) is ALL NSF COVID funding, not RAPID-only.

### 1.4 Convergence Accelerator
Launched 2019; Phase 1 up to $750K, Phase 2 up to $5M. **No track dedicated to pandemics/infectious disease** (negative finding); closest are individual chemical-sensing projects. NSF's dedicated pandemic vehicle is PIPP.

### 1.5 Expeditions in Computing / AI
Center-scale CISE program (up to $15M/7yr). Most relevant: **"Global Pervasive Computational Epidemiology"** (~$10M/5yr, 2020), lead PI Madhav Marathe (UVA Biocomplexity Institute). No AI Institute dedicated specifically to epidemiology.

### 1.6 Biology Integration Institutes (BII) & MIDAS distinction
BIIs (NSF 20-508, from FY2020; up to $12.5M/5yr; 14+ institutes). Most disease-relevant: **VERENA (Viral Emergence Research Initiative), Georgetown University**. **MIDAS is NIH/NIGMS** (began May 2004) — not NSF.
