# JPEO-CBRND — Joint Program Executive Office for CBRN Defense

## Introduction
JPEO-CBRND is DoD's lead for development, acquisition, fielding, and life-cycle support of CBRN defense equipment and **medical countermeasures** (vaccines, therapeutics, diagnostics). Reports through the Army's ASA(ALT); HQ Aberdeen Proving Ground, MD. On the DoD acquisition side it is the closest analogue to BARDA — and the two frequently **co-fund/co-contract** the same products. Most prominently, during COVID-19 JPEO-CBRND (via the Army Contracting Command and the MCDC OTA) served as the **contracting vehicle** that obligated billions of HHS/BARDA dollars for Operation Warp Speed. Awardees are **representative**; COVID-era figures are largely **joint BARDA/DoD** and should not be double-counted against BARDA.

## Section 1 — Programmes and Acquisition Mechanisms

### 1.1 MCM-relevant structure
- **Joint Project Manager for CBRN Medical (JPM CBRN Medical)** — MCM programs; houses **RAIDR** (Rapid Acquisition and Investigation of Drugs for Repurposing).
- **Joint Project Lead for CBRND Enabling Biotechnologies (JPL CBRND EB)** — platform tech; flagship **MCMPT (Medical Countermeasures Platform Technologies)**. >28 National Stock Numbers sponsored.

### 1.2 Platform / rapid-response programmes
MCMPT (reusable platforms); RAIDR (drug repurposing); **RAPID** (pre-positioned MCM library); **VAMP** (Vaccine Acceleration by Modular Progression); **GUIDE** (AI/ML drug engineering). Collaboration with Vanderbilt UMC Vaccine Center on antibody discovery. "Speed over perfection," drawing on COVID lessons.

### 1.3 Medical CBRN Defense Consortium (MCDC) OTA
JPEO-CBRND's principal flexible acquisition vehicle. An **Other Transaction Agreement** consortium (industry/academia/small-biz/non-profit) operating outside the FAR. Established ~**2015**; managed by **Advanced Technology International (ATI)**. **~$10B ceiling, ~20-yr term, >$7.8B awarded** (industry-sourced/approximate). Scope: diagnostics, vaccines, therapeutics, autoinjectors, biosurveillance, manufacturing. Kaléo's naloxone autoinjector received the inaugural MCDC Innovation Award.

### 1.4 DoD Advanced Development and Manufacturing (ADM) capability
Government-sponsored, contractor-operated biologics plant in **Alachua, FL**. 2013: Nanotherapeutics awarded original contract; 2017: opened (rebranded **Ology Bioservices**, 183,000 sq ft); Apr 2021: acquired by **National Resilience**; expanded under a JPEO-CBRND cost-share (capacity >doubled, fill/finish online by end-2023).

### 1.5 COVID-19 / Operation Warp Speed contracting role
Best-documented function as a large-scale acquirer and clearest example of **DoD-as-contracting-vehicle for HHS/BARDA money**. JPEO-CBRND partnered with **Army Contracting Command** (Joint COVID-19 Response Division) using FAR + OTA contracts; the **Joint Assisted Acquisition (JA2)** team had pre-existing contracts with J&J, Moderna, AstraZeneca, Pfizer. Per JPEO/Army reporting: facilitated **up to ~$10.8B for OWS**, ~$1B for the Defense Health Agency, and >$455M for DoD — but the OWS/DHA dollars were **HHS/BARDA and DHA funds**.

## Section 2 — Representative Awardees

### 2.1 COVID-19 vaccines (OWS — contracted via DoD/ACC, funded largely by BARDA)
Moderna (~$1.53B Aug 2020; ~$2.48B total incl. $483M+$472M dev); Pfizer/BioNTech (~$1.95B advance purchase); Janssen/J&J (~$1B + ~$456M); AstraZeneca (up to ~$1.2B); **Novavax ($1.6B, largest single OWS vaccine award**, via the COVID-19 vaccine base OTA mechanism); Sanofi/GSK (~$2.1B). *Funding source = HHS/BARDA; contracting vehicle = DoD (JPEO-CBRND/ACC). Both descriptions are correct.*

### 2.2 COVID-19 therapeutics / mAbs (via MCDC OTA / DoD + BARDA)
**Regeneron** — REGN-COV2, **$450.2M max** (Jul 2020), explicitly an "MCDC Agreement" (BARDA + DoD JPEO-CBRND + ACC) — cleanest MCDC example. **AstraZeneca** — AZD7442 (Evusheld), ~**$486M** (BARDA + DoD; OTA dated Oct 28, 2020; an earlier ~$157M dev figure also circulated). Eli Lilly/Merck — additional OWS mAb/antiviral awards (routing through JPEO vs direct BARDA not confirmed).

### 2.3 COVID-19 diagnostics
**Cepheid** — rapid molecular SARS-CoV-2 test (GeneXpert); BARDA provided ~$3.7M leveraging an **existing Cepheid–JPEO-CBRND agreement**; later multiplex flu/RSV/SARS-CoV-2 test backed by BARDA + JPEO-CBRND.

### 2.4 ADM partners
Nanotherapeutics / Ology Bioservices (built/operated DoD-ADM); National Resilience (acquired Ology Apr 2021; expanded under JPEO cost-share).

### 2.5 Core (non-COVID) CBRN MCM awardees
Kaléo (inaugural MCDC Innovation Award, naloxone autoinjector); Vanderbilt UMC Vaccine Center (antibody discovery). Smallpox/anthrax MCM contracts (Emergent ACAM2000/AV7909, Bavarian Nordic JYNNEOS, Chimerix TEMBEXA) sit at the BARDA/ASPR–DoD interface — the large stockpile contracts (e.g. Emergent ~$2.8B ACAM2000) were **ASPR/BARDA**, not JPEO (flagged).

## Key clarifications
JPEO-CBRND is DoD's acquisition counterpart to BARDA; the MCDC OTA is the workhorse (~$10B ceiling, >$7.8B awarded). During COVID it was primarily a **contracting vehicle for HHS/BARDA money** (~$10.8B OWS + ~$1B DHA + ~$455M DoD) — **do not add to BARDA totals**. Cleanest MCDC awards: Regeneron ($450.2M), AstraZeneca AZD7442 (~$486M), Cepheid (~$3.7M). Uncertain: 2013 Nanotherapeutics ADM value, ADM cost-share amounts, MCDC ceiling/cumulative (industry-sourced), AZ split, Lilly/Merck routing.
