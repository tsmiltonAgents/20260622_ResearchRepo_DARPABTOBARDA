# Grants & Contracts Awarded Under Pandemic-Relevant DARPA BTO and BARDA Programmes

> **Phase 2 of the research project.** This file inventories the grants, contracts, cooperative agreements, OTA awards, and advance-purchase agreements made under the programmes flagged as pandemic / infectious-disease relevant in [`allprogs.md`](allprogs.md).
>
> Research compiled **2026-06-22** via parallel web-research agents drawing on agency press releases (DARPA, HHS/ASPR, medicalcountermeasures.gov, NIH/NIAID), recipient/company press releases, SEC filings, USAspending.gov / FPDS, GAO reports, and trade press.

## How to read this file

- **Ceiling vs obligated.** Government awards are routinely a small base award plus large option ceilings. Headline figures are usually "up to" / total-potential, not money actually obligated. This distinction is noted per row where known.
- **Confidence.** No awards were invented. Where a figure or attribution is uncertain it is flagged. Where a programme's full performer roster is likely larger than what is public, that is stated rather than filled in.
- **Scope boundaries.** Common mis-attributions (awards often wrongly linked to these programmes) are listed under "Boundary guards" so they are NOT double-counted.
- **Federal databases caveat.** USAspending.gov / FPDS / GovTribe detail pages render dynamically and largely resisted automated extraction; many disclosed dollars come from press releases. For audit-grade obligated dollars, query FPDS/USAspending by the cited PIIDs and consult the GAO reports named below.

**DARPA BTO** — *Therapeutics & Prevention cluster:* P3, ADEPT (therapeutics), PREEMPT, INTERCEPT, PREPARE, Prophecy, THoR, HEALR, ReVector, NOW, AIM. *Diagnostics & Detection cluster:* DIGET, Friend or Foe, ECHO, ADEPT (diagnostics), Bio-Attribution Challenge, AMPHORA, Prometheus.
