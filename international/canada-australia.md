# Canada & Australia — Government Funders of Pandemic Preparedness and Medical Countermeasures

Principal Canadian and Australian government funders of pandemic preparedness, infectious-disease R&D, biosecurity, and MCMs. CAD≈0.73 USD; AUD≈0.65 USD (indicative). Many figures are "up to" ceilings or have confidential commercial components; representative, not exhaustive; uncertain figures flagged.

## 1. Programmes / Funders

### CANADA
- **CIHR** (Canadian Institutes of Health Research) — federal health-research agency; ran COVID **Rapid Research Funding** rounds (CAD 55.3M/100 projects Mar 2020; CAD 111.1M/140 projects June 2020; CAD 25.2M Mar 2021), a Variants of Concern initiative, and a CAD 250M Clinical Trials Fund (Budget 2021).
- **PHAC / National Microbiology Laboratory (NML)** — Canada's only **BSL-4** (Winnipeg, opened 1999); ~CAD 80M, ~550 staff (2018-19); Ebola/Marburg/Lassa work. (2019–24 Winnipeg lab security scandal noted as context only.)
- **Strategic Innovation Fund (SIF)** — flagship industrial-investment programme; Budget 2021 dedicated **CAD 1B over 7 yrs** to life sciences/biomanufacturing.
- **Biomanufacturing and Life Sciences Strategy (2021)** — **CAD 2.2B over 7 yrs** to rebuild domestic biomanufacturing (SIF + VIDO + NRC Biologics Manufacturing Centre + Canada Biomedical Research Fund + Clinical Trials Fund).
- **IDRC** (International Development Research Centre) — >CAD 70M pandemic response in LMICs since 2020; ~CAD 20M One Health.
- **CanSino/NRC (2020, failed)** — ~CAD 44M Montréal facility upgrade; China blocked vaccine material; abandoned Aug 2020.
- **CEPI/COVAX** — CAD 90M to CEPI; >CAD 840M to COVAX Facility.

### AUSTRALIA
- **NHMRC** (National Health and Medical Research Council) — primary health-research funder (~AUD 1B/yr); funds the **APPRISE** network (AUD 5M 2016–2021 + AUD 2M COVID top-up).
- **MRFF** (Medical Research Future Fund) — **AUD 25.4B** capital-preserved endowment; 3rd 10-Year Plan directs **AUD 6.5B** across ~10 Missions (incl. Genomics Health Futures → pathogen genomics); ~AUD 96M for COVID research; AUD 750M Clinical Trials Activity stream.
- **CSIRO / Australian Centre for Disease Preparedness (ACDP)** — Geelong **BSL-4** zoonotic facility (formerly AAHL); **AUD 220M** upgrade (2020) + AUD 38M Stage 2.
- **Doherty Institute** — Univ. Melbourne/RMH joint venture; isolated SARS-CoV-2 early 2020; hosts APPRISE; mainly NHMRC-funded.
- **mRNA manufacturing** — Moderna (Monash, Victoria; up to 100M doses/yr; up to AUD 25M Commonwealth + AUD 12.3M Victoria, rest confidential); BioNTech (Melbourne).
- **Australian Centre for Disease Control (CDC)** — new agency, **AUD 251.7M over 4 yrs**; interim from Jan 2024, permanent body from 1 Jan 2026.
- **CEPI** — Australia is a donor (CEPI is HQ'd in Oslo, not host); **AUD 100M** pledge to the 100 Days Mission (2022).

## 2. Grantees / Awardees (representative)

**Canada (Strategic Innovation Fund unless noted):** Sanofi Pasteur Toronto (up to CAD 415M, 2021, flu vaccine facility); AbCellera (CAD 175.6M 2020 + CAD 225M 2023); National Resilience Mississauga (up to CAD 199.16M, 2021); Medicago Québec (up to CAD 173M +27M; *later wound down 2023*); KABS Labs (CAD 54.2M); BIOVECTRA (CAD 39.8M); Novocol (CAD 32.7M); Precision NanoSystems (CAD 18.2M +25.1M); VIDO-InterVac/U Saskatchewan (CAD 35M + 59.2M); NRC Biologics Manufacturing Centre Montréal (CAD 126M); Genome Canada CanCOGeN (CAD 40M). *(Moderna Canada plant incentive undisclosed.)*

**Australia:** CSL Seqirus (~AUD 1B, 2020, 10-yr onshore flu-pandemic vaccine/antivenom mfg, Tullamarine; opened Dec 2025); Moderna Victoria mRNA facility (up to ~AUD 25M Commonwealth + 12.3M Victoria); Doherty Institute (>AUD 30.5M NHMRC Investigator Grants); University of Queensland molecular clamp (CEPI US$10.6M 2019; Clamp2 US$5.7M 2022); AusPathoGen pathogen genomics (AUD 10M, MRFF); APPRISE (AUD 5M +2M).

## Key flags
Canadian SIF data is well-consolidated (ISED backgrounder). Cautionary cases: Medicago (wound down 2023) and CanSino/NRC (CAD 44M, no doses). Uncertain: Moderna Canada incentive undisclosed; Moderna/BioNTech Australia deals partly confidential; UQ figures are CEPI USD with approx AUD. CEPI is HQ'd in Oslo (Australia is a donor, not host).
